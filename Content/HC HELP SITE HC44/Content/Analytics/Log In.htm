Logging In To HC Standard® Analytics

To launch HC Standard®, open any web browser and enter the organization's access URL into the navigation bar.

  • HC Standard® URL: ______________________________________

Each organization is provided a unique access URL. If the access URL is unknown, please contact GER Technical Support by calling 1.866.242.4035 or by emailing support@ger911.com.

On the HC Standard® login dialog, as shown in FIGURE 1-301:

  1. Type the valid Username and Password, provided by an Admin.
  2. Click or tap the Sign In button.

FIGURE 1-301: Login Dialog

Access the HC Analytics link, as shown in FIGURE 1-302:

  1. Click or tap the Workspaces button.
  2. Click or tap HC Analytics workspace.
  3. Click or tap the HC Analytics link.

FIGURE 1-302: Access HC Analytics

Application Menu

At the top of the HC Analytics window is a navigation bar to let the user know where they are in the system. At any point, the user may use this navigation bar to open the application menu to access an HC Analytics application, as shown in FIGURE 1-303:

  1. Click or tap the APPS link in the navigation bar.
