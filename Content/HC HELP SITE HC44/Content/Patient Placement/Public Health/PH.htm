Public Health Users

Access the desired role from the Home screen, as shown in FIGURE 5-1:

  1. Click or tap the Public Health organization type.
  2. Select the Organization.
  3. Click or tap the Proceed with Training/Exercise button to use HC Standard® Patient Placement in training mode or click or tap the Proceed with Real World Event button to use HC Standard® Patient Placement in live mode.

FIGURE 5-1: Home

The Public Health Dashboard will display, as shown in FIGURE 5-2. The dashboard contains navigation options and summary chart data. The Patient Tracking System button will log the user into HC Standard to view patient tracking data collected from HC Patient Tracking™.
