Using Display

The Display button is used to change what columns display.

To display only a subset of measures or change measure order, as shown in FIGURE 6-4:

  1. Click or tap the Display button.
  2. Click or tap the square to the left of each measure to be shown or temporarily hidden from display. A checked square indicates that the measure will be displayed. The absence of a checkmark indicates that the measure will be hidden from display.
