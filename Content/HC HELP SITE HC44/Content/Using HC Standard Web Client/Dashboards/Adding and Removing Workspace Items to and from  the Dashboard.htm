Adding Items to a Dashboard

The process for adding workspace items as dashboard items is the same way for all. In the series of examples below, an Announcement is being added as a dashboard item.

To add an announcement to a dashboard, as shown in FIGURE 4-101:

  1. Click or tap the Announcement to be added to a dashboard.
  2. Click or tap the Tools button.

FIGURE 4-101: Tools Button

Continue adding the item(s) to a dashboard, as shown in FIGURE 4-102:

  1. Click or tap the circle of the Dashboard that will receive the Announcement.
  2. Click or tap the Continue button to confirm.

FIGURE 4-102: Add Announcement to Dashboard

Another way to add an item to a dashboard is from the Workspace Overview, as seen in FIGURE 4-103

  1. Click or tap the gauge icon to the left of the item name.

FIGURE 4-103: Add To Dashboard - Workspace Overview

Continue adding the item(s) to a dashboard, as shown in FIGURE 4-104:

  1. Click or tap the circle of the Dashboard that will receive the Announcement.
  2. Click or tap the Continue button to confirm.

FIGURE 4-104: Add Item to Dashboard

Removing Items from a Dashboard

To remove an item from the user dashboard, as shown in FIGURE 4-105:

  1. Click or tap the trashcan icon next to the dashboard item's name.

FIGURE 4-105: Remove dashboard item
