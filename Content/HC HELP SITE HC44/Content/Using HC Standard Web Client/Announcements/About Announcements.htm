Announcements

Announcements are notices that are posted on a bulletin board within a Workspace.

Some announcements may have an expiration date. In such cases, the announcement will be deleted from the workspace on a pre-determined date. At this time, only HC StandardÂ® Administrators may create an announcement and an expiration date (if there is one).

Viewing an Announcement

To view an announcement within a workspace, as shown in FIGURE 4-8:

  1. Click or tap the Announcement tab in the Workspace Item menu.
  2. Click or tap the Name of the desired announcement.
  3. The Announcement will open. Each Announcement has a title area and some text below the title.

Depending on permissions, announcements may be added or deleted.

FIGURE 4-8: Announcements

Adding an Announcement to a Dashboard

To add an announcement to a dashboard, as shown in FIGURE 4-9:

  1. Click or tap the Announcement to be added to a dashboard.
  2. Click or tap the Tools button.

FIGURE 4-9: Tools Button

To continue adding the item(s) to a dashboard, as shown in FIGURE 4-10:

  1. Click or tap the circle of the Dashboard that will receive the Announcement.
  2. Click or tap the Continue button to confirm.
