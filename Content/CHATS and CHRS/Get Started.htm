About HC Standard® Hospital Alert Tracking

HC Standard® Hospital Alert Tracking provides facility status tracking with an integrated request system.

HC Hospital Alert Tracking includes a public and private interface showing overall status of different facilities and the time elapsed while on a particular status. Authorized users have the ability to manage status requests for their facility(ies). HC Hospital Alert Tracking includes several reports that may be run on a single or group of facilities.

EMRC allows authorized administrators to approve or deny requests made by facilities.

Access is determined by an HC Standard® Service Admin, and may only be changed by an Admin.
