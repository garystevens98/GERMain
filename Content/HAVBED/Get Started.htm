About HC Standard® HAvBED

The HC Standard® HAvBED Module provides the ability to gather bed availability from a state or region and quickly and accurately submit it to the Department of Health and Human Services’ (HHS) National Hospital Available Beds for Emergencies and Disasters (HAvBED) System.

The HC Standard® system allows users to send notifications to facilities requesting a bed status update. Once the updates are received, the data is compiled and put into an XML format for submission to HHS. The system provides a history of submissions for access later. This document will cover how to request bed updates, how to respond to a request, and how to submit the data to HHS. Access is determined by an HC Standard®Service Admin, and may only be changed by an Admin.

For more information on the HHS HAvBED System, please visit https://havbed.hhs.gov.
