Timeline

Introduction

The timeline shows data in a time-based view. Objects of different types are ordered in time to see the progression through past, present, and future.

Access the Timeline app from the application menu, as shown in FIGURE 1-1:

  1. Click or tap the Timeline button.

Application Menu - Timeline

Timeline View

The Timeline app shows objects in a time-ordered list, from the future at the top to the past at the bottom. By default when the user enters the view the current date/time will be at the very top of the view. Scroll down to see past events, scroll up to see future events.

FIGURE 1-2 shows the timeline view:

  1. Each event block has the date/ time, the object time, the object name or title, and a picture if applicable.
  2. The icons in the middle represent the type - a purple clock for Event, a red warning triangle for Incident, a green checklist page for Assessment or Field Interview.
  3. Access the item's profile page by clicking or tapping the item name.

Threat Streams

The timeline toolbar provides the following buttons, shown in FIGURE 1-3:

  1. Now - moves the timeline to display the current date/time in the center.
  2. Settings - displays options to search and filter the timeline.

Timline Toolbar

Filter The Timeline

To filter the timeline, as shown in FIGURE 1-3:

  1. Click or tap an Settings button.

Settings button

The settings will appear, as shown in FIGURE 1-5:

  1. Enter a Start Date and/or End Date.
  2. Add tags, if desired.
  3. Check or Uncheck the types of objects to display.
  4. Click or tap Save to save the filter.

    OR

  5. Click or tap Close to cancel changes.
