About HC Standard® Analytics

The HC Standard® Analytics Module is a cloud-based multi-tenant multi-app platform for incident management and threat intelligence. It contains a collection of apps to support public safety and security workflows to track assets, perform assessments, manage incidents, view prioritized threat information, and see it all on a map or in a timeline. The underlying platform supports API layers for integration, and a stream processing engine to ingest and score threat and news content.
