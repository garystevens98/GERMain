Bluetooth Print

Connecting a Bluetooth Printer and Printing

From within an Individual patient record or from a Group triage list, a user can choose to print a basic patient profile or a patient group manifest.

Bluetooth Printing Window

 

 

When the Bluetooth Printing window first appears, as shown in FIGURE 1-1, it will automatically search for any Bluetooth printer within range of the handheld.

  1. If a printer was not immediately found, tap Refresh to search again.
  2. Once one is found, it will be displayed in the Connect to a Bluetooth Printer list. Select a printer.
  3. Choose the Printer Model.
  4. Tap Print to print.
  5. Tap Close to exit Bluetooth Printing.
