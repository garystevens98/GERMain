Granting Access to Patient Archive Workspace

Administrators may decide to elect other users to manage and archive patient data. In order to grant permission to access the Patient Archive workspace, Administrators need to add the user(s) to the Patient Archive Access user group.

Only user accounts designated with HC Standard® Administrator permissions will have access to HC Standard® Admin mode. In the example below, the user is entering Admin mode. To access HC Standard® Admin mode, as shown in FIGURE 1-1:

  1. Click or tap the User icon.
  2. Click or tap the Admin mode option located in the drop-down menu.

Admin mode in User Tab

Open user groups, as shown in FIGURE 1-2:

  1. Click or tap on the Users, and Permissions icon.
  2. Click or tap on the Groups icon under the Manage My Users section.

Groups

Access the Patient Archive Access user group, as shown in FIGURE 1-3:

  1. Type Patient Archive into the search bar to filter the list of user groups.
  2. Double click or tap the Patient Archive Access user group.

Patient Archive Access User Group

The Patient Archive Access user group will open, as shown in FIGURE 1-4. Add users to this group to grant access to the Patient Archive Workspace.

  1. Click or tap the Users tab.
  2. Click the Add Users button.

Add Users

Add desired users, as shown in FIGURE 1-5:

  1. Click or tap the circle to the left of the desired user to place a check mark.
  2. Click or tap the Add Selected button.
  3. Click or tap the Save button when complete.
