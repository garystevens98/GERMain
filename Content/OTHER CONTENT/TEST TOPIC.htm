How to Quickly View a PCR

 

We created this feature so that you would be able to view PCRs as quickly as possible.

 

Step-by-Step

 

1. Once you are on the home screen for Cloud PCR, you can click on the Actions button that is located next to the PCR that you would like to view. 

 

2. Once you click on the Actions button, a list of options will appear. Go ahead and click Quick View to get a small preview of the details in the PCR.

 

 

3. This is the view you receive under Quick View. There is also an option to print or download the form.
