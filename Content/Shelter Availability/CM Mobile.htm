Facility Management Screen

Upon first logging into the mobile app, users will be presented with the Facility Management screen as shown in FIGURE 1-1. From here, users will see:

  1. Facility Nameincluding date and time of Last Update
  2. Shelter Information which includes Total Rooms, Total Beds, Available Beds, and Occupied Beds
  3. Unique ID Lookup field, Add New Room button, and Scan Room button

Adding a New Room

Users with appropriate administrative privileges will be able to add a new room directly from the mobile app. following the instructions below:

  1. Click the Add New Room button or select Add Room in the Hamburger Menu

Add New Room Buttons

  1. Fill in new room information on the Add New Room screen

Add New Room screen

  1. If you want to add images, press the down caret next to Images Upload
  2. Press the Take A Photo button to take photos with your phone's default camera app
  3. Image previews will appear below the Take a Photo box
  4. When you have added all of the room info and photos, press the Add Room button

Add Images

  1. After adding the new room, you will see the confirmation message shown in FIGURE 1-3
  2. From here, you can use the buttons to Go To Dashboard or Add New Room

Updating Room Information

Users with appropriate administrative priveleges can change the room information from within the mobile app. Fields that can be updated are Available Beds, Room Status, and Images. To update these metrics, follow the steps below:

  1. Press the Scan Room button to open the camera and scan the unique room ID

Scan Room

  1. Alternately, you may manually type the ID into the Unique Room ID field
  2. The Scan Room button will change to Find Room; press this to look up the room by the typed ID

Manual Room Lookup

  1. Use the listed fields to update Num Beds Available and Room Status
  2. Press the down caret next to Images Upload to access the Take A Photo button and add photos
  3. When complete, press the Update Room button

Edit Room Screen

  1. After updating the room, you will see the confirmation message in FIGURE 1-8. Use the button to Go To Dashboard.
