HC Evacuee Tracking™ Module (ETM) Overview

Global Emergency Response's Evacuee Tracking™ (ETM) is a module of HC Mobile. It is an integrated mobile solution for electronic tracking of evacuees in and out of shelter and staging locations. It allows near real-time data transmission from remote locations and seamlessly integrates with HC Standard® Patient Tracking.

Key benefits of PTS include:

  • Near real-time transmission of shelter data to a command center.
  • Barcode identification and scanning of evacuee.
  • Tracking of evacuee special needs information.
  • GPS and Date/Time stamping of data entry events.
  • Audio and video recording capability.
  • HIPAA and HITECH compliant. Data is encrypted on the device while at rest and in transit.
