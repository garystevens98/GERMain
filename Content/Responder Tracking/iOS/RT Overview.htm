Responder Tracking Overview

Global Emergency Resources’ Responder Tracking™ is a module of HC Mobile. It is an integrated mobile solution to tracking responders during any scenario. It allows near real-time data transmission and seamlessly integrates with HC Standard®.

Key benefits of Responder Tracking include:

  • Near real-time transmission of responder status to a command center.
  • Tracking of responder mission assignment and task order status.
  • GPS and Date/Time stamping of data entry events.
  • HIPAA and HITECH compliant.
  • Data is encrypted on the device while at rest and in transit.
