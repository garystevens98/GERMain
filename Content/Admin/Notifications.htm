Notifications

Notification tools aid in the creating and managing of messages from the Admin. Messages can be created, saved as templates, and sent to any combination of users and groups via HC Standard®, e-mail or SMS text.

To manage notifications, as shown in FIGURE 1-1:

  1. Click or tap on the Notifications & Templates icon.
  2. Click or tap on the Notifications icon under the Manage My Notifications section.

Notifications

FIGURE 1-2 shows the main notifications page. This page lists all notifications previously created and sent. It also allows for a new notification to be sent.

Sending Notifications

A notification can be sent to any combination of groups, workspaces and users. When sending a notification to a group or workspace, all users in the group or workspace are notified. The basic steps to create a new notification involve:

  • Creating the notification content.
  • Choosing the groups, users and workspaces that should receive the notification.
  • Sending the notification.

To create a new notification, as shown in FIGURE 1-3:

  1. Click or tap the Send button.

Send Notification Button

FIGURE 1-9 shows the Create Notification dialog.

  1. Click or tap the Start Wizard button.

Create Notification

Enter the notification options, as shown in FIGURE 1-10:

  1. UncheckRequires user input.
  2. Select the Notification Type.
  3. Enter the Notification Subject.
  4. Enter the Message.
  5. Click or tap the Next button.

Create New Notification - Step 1

Sending a Notification with a request for Access Rule-based input

One of the notification options are to use Access Rules which allows certain users to only edit certain entities in a notification request. If Access Rules have been configured by an administrator, then users with the appropriate permissions may send Access Rule-based notifications to other users.

To send a notification that requires user input to update based on an Access Rule, as shown in FIGURE 1-18:

  1. Click or tap the Notification button.
  2. Click or tap the Send Notification button.

Other Notification Action(s)

Other actions may be performed with notifications including sending a notification and canceling a notification.

To perform any of the above actions on one or more notifications, as shown in FIGURE 1-7:

Actions Button

Select the desired action and follow the prompt, as shown in FIGURE 1-8.

  1. Resend Notification will resend the notification to those users on the original distribution who have not yet acknowledged it.
  2. Cancel Notification will send the notification to everyone on the original distribution with "Canceled" added to the subject header.

Filtering Notifications

The main Notifications page may contain many notification listings. Pre-determined filter tools are available to sort and filter the list.

To filter notifications, as shown in FIGURE 1-9:

  1. Click or tap the Filter button.
  2. Select the desired Filter from the drop-down menu.

To remove the filter:

  1. Click or tap the Filter item listed in the drop-down menu called Filter.

Notification Templates

Notification Templates help to organize and manage Notifications. Use the Templates tool to create a notification that will be sent at a later time or be re-used over time.

To manage notification templates, as shown in FIGURE 1-10:

  1. Click or tap on the Notifications & Templates icon.
  2. Click or tap on the Templates icon under the Manage My Notifications section.

Creating a Notification Template

To create a notification template, as shown in FIGURE 1-11:

  1. Click or tap the Create New Template button.

Create New Template Button

The Add Template tool will display with the Details tab already selected, as shown in FIGURE 1-12.

  1. Enter a Subject.
  2. Enter a Message.
  3. Set a due date.  This is optional and will automatically expire and remove the notification after the due date has passed.
  4. Enable or disable Track Acknowledgment to track users that received the notification. This forces a notification received message window to continuously pop-up on a user's screen until the user clicks or taps the acknowledge button.
