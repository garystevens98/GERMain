Troubleshooting the EWRAP™

Troubleshooting the EWRAP™

Symptom Possible Solution
  • Unit makes a hissing sound while charging
  • Turn off and unplug unit from AC power.
  • Contact GER Technical Support immediately.

  • Unit has a strong sulfur odor during charging
  • Turn off and unplug unit from AC power.
  • Contact GER Technical Support immediately.

  • Liquid is coming out of the unit

  • Turn off and unplug unit from AC power.

  • Wear PPE and use baking soda to neutralize any liquid.

  • Contact GER Technical Support.
  • 12v Power Plug does not work
  • Verify the 7.5 amp fuse inside the unit has not been blown.

  • Unit will not charge

  • Verify the 7.5 amp fuse inside the unit has not been blown.

  • Contact GER Technical Support.

  • EWRAP unit will not connect to the Internet

  • Make sure unit has power.

  • Turn green switch to the On position.

  • Wait several minutes.

  • Once the device emits one high-pitched chirp followed by two high-­pitched chips it is ready for use.

  • If the device still will not connect to the Internet, contact GER Technical Support.

  • Unit will not turn on

  • Plug unit into AC power.

  • Verify charger is working correctly.

  • The unit may be run while plugged into AC power, if the charger does not exhibit any problems.

  • Unit is warm while charging
  • Open case to provide ventilation while charging.
