Links

Links allow for the posting of links to websites that may be needed by other users throughout the HC Standard® System.

Links are helpful in providing quick access to current web resources such as traffic reports, weather conditions, live video feeds, and local news reports. At this time, only Admins may create a link.

 

Note: Pop-up settings need to be enabled for the HC Standard® URL. To view all links, enable Mixed Content setting in the user's browser settings.

Viewing a Link

To view a link within a Workspace:

  • Click or tap on the Link tab in the Workspace Item menu.
  • Click or tap on the desired Link.
  • Depending on how the link was configured, it may launch in a new window, or display in HC Standard®.

Adding a Link to a Dashboard

To add a link to a dashboard, FIGURE 1-1:

  1. Click or tap the Link to be added to a dashboard.
  2. Click or tap the Tools button.

Tools Button

To continue adding the item(s) to a dashboard, as shown in FIGURE 1-2:

  1. Click or tap the circle of the Dashboard that will receive the Link.
  2. Click or tap the Continue button to confirm.
