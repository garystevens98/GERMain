Editing a Record

Certain actions in HC Standard® Patient Placement will require users to edit patient records. To edit a patient record, as shown in FIGURE 1-1:

  1. Click or tap the check box to the left of the desired Patient ID.
  2. Enter information as necessary in the white cells.  Cells that are not white are not editable.
  3. Click or tap Save to save the changes.
  4. Click or tap Cancel to cancel changes.
