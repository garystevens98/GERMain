If there is an evacuation

Individual facilities must make their own decision to evacuate – we cannot so we will not make that decision for them.

The burden in this scenario is on THE EVACUATING FACILITY.

Evacuating facilities:

  • Decide which patient/resident moves when and where (with consent from receiving facilities)

  • Must find appropriate transport for evacuees

  • Are responsible for paying bills associated with the evacuation
