Placement Boards

Each facility should have generic facility-specific logins. Multiple people CAN log in with a single account simultaneously. However, if the wrong Username/Password is entered incorrectly three times in a row, that Username will become locked out. That’s why it is good to have a backup Username/Password. GER can unlock the account, if you are not able to get a hold of Support Desk, contact your HCF for assistance.

 

Enter your facility-specific Username, Password and click Sign In
