State Wide Requests

Receiving Facilities are able to place offers to accept patients from evacuating facilities. Patients entered into HC Standard® Patient Placement from evacuating facilities will be displayed under the State Wide Requests. To view and accept patients, as shown in FIGURE 1-1:

  1. Click or tap either the View Acute Care Requests or View Long Term Care Requests button.

State Wide Requests Buttons

The page will display and contains the following options, as shown in FIGURE 1-3:

  1. Quick Filters - used to quickly change the display of patients, see "Using Quick Filters" on page 1.
  2. Display - used to adjust the columns seen in the display, see "Using Display" on page 1.
  3. Column Sorting and Filtering - used to sort and/or filter the patient display, see "Sorting Patients" on page 1. Users may also organize column order by clicking and dragging the column headers into the desired location in the grid.

State Wide Requests

Offering to Accept Patients

A receiving facility that has the capacity to receive patients may make an offer to accept a patient, as shown in FIGURE 1-3:

  1. Click or tap the check mark next to the desired patient.
  2. Click or tap the Confirm button to place an offer.
  3. To rescind an offer, click or tap the Rescind button.

Note: When a patient receives three offers, no additional offers may be made to the patient.
