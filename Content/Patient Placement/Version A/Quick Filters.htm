Using Quick Filters

Quick filters are the drop-downs at the top of a record view page. These easy access items allow a user to quickly look for certain records according to a particular criteria. To use quick filters, as shown in FIGURE 1-1:

  1. Click or tap a drop-down menu to select a choice to filter. Some lists will allow multiple items to be selected.
  2. Click or tap the Clear Filters button to clear filters added by the user and return to the initial record view.
