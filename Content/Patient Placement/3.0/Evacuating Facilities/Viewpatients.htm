View Patients

Evacuating Facility users may view all of their facility's patients and assign patients to other facilities from the Patients screen. To enter View Patients, as shown in FIGURE 1-1:

  1. Select the Patients option on the top menu.
  2. Select the All Patients tab

View Patients ButtonView Patients

Assigning Patients to a Facility

Patients that have received facility offers may be assigned to a facility. Facilities may assign, or unassign patients as needed. To assign a patient to a facility, as shown in FIGURE 1-3:

  1. Select the building icon corresponding to the patient
  2. Select the facility the patient is to be assigned to

Assign Patients

Un-assign patients by following the same steps above and selecting Unassign .

Viewing Facility Contact Information

Users may view contact information for Receiving Facilities, as shown in FIGURE 1-4:

  1. Select the exclamation icon next to the Facility name under Facilities Can Receive columns.
