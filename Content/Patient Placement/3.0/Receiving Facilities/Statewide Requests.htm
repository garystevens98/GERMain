State Wide Requests

Receiving facilities are able to place offers to accept patients from evacuating facilities. Patients entered into HC Standard® Patient Placement from evacuating facilities will be displayed under the State Wide Requests. To view and accept patients, as shown in FIGURE 1-1:

  1. Select Requests on the top menu
  2. Select the Acute Care Requests or Long Term Care Requests tab.

State Wide Requests Buttons

Offering to Accept Patients

A receiving facility that has the capacity to receive patients may make an offer to accept a patient, as shown in FIGURE 1-3:

  1. Select the person icon next to the desired patient.
  2. Verify that the correct contact information is correct, update as needed.
  3. Select Clear to erase the Contact Info. These values are required to proceed.
  4. Select Confirm to add your facility to the list of facilities that can receive this patient.

Accept Patients

To Rescind an offer:

  1. Select the person icon next to the desired patient.
  2. Verify that the correct contact information is correct, update as needed.
  3. Select Update to save any changes made to the Contact Information.
  4. Select Rescind.

.

 

Note: When a patient receives three offers, no additional offers may be made to the patient.
